Visualizing Temperature by Latitude

Using a scatterplot, we're able to see how temperature changes as we move closer to the equator. It's not a suprise to see that temperatures rise the close one is to the equator. This scatterplot has a fairly uniform, bell-shaped curve. Beyond showing temperature trends, this scatterplot also gives us some insight into the distribution of cities across the globe. This graph suggests that there's a great concentration of cities in the northern hemisphere.
